Why Choose Thulir Construction?

We don't just build structures—we create lasting value through innovation, quality, and unmatched attention to detail.

01

Anti-Termite Treatment

We carry out comprehensive anti-termite treatment after soil filling up to the basement level to ensure long-term protection against termites. Your investment deserves lasting protection from the ground up.

Long-term Protection
02

Detailed Agreement Copy

We provide a comprehensive agreement copy to clients before starting work, including all specifications, details of work, and the payment schedule. Complete transparency from day one—no hidden surprises.

100% Transparency
03

Structural Analysis & Elevation

We deliver a detailed structural analysis and share the exterior elevation photo as discussed with the client before completing the basement work. Visualize your dream before we build it.

Preview Your Vision
04

Water Leakage Testing

We conduct thorough water leakage tests in sunken areas and RCC water tanks on raw concrete surfaces before any plastering. After identifying leakage spots, we perform 'pressure PU grout' to arrest the leakage, followed by regular plastering work.

Zero Leakage Guarantee
05

Mini Mixer Machine for Plaster Mortar

We use a mini mixer machine to prepare plaster mortar, ensuring proper and consistent cement mixing for superior quality. Modern equipment for flawless finishes.

Superior Quality
06

Use of Vibrators

We ensure the use of vibrators for all major concrete work to avoid honeycombs and achieve uniform settlement of concrete in the formwork. Professional-grade equipment for structural integrity.

Structural Excellence
07

Mini Power Trowel Machine

We use a mini power trowel machine across plastered surfaces to ensure uniform finishes and an aesthetically pleasing look. Precision finishing for a flawless appearance.

Perfect Finish
08

Putty Spraying Machine

We utilize a putty spraying machine with a 5-foot-long trowel to level surfaces, eliminating unwanted undulations on walls and achieving a smooth and level finish.

Smooth Surfaces
09

Crane for Material Shifting

Our crane, operable up to 5 floors, eliminates labor wastage and saves time in shifting materials to higher floors. Efficient logistics for faster project completion.

Time Efficiency
10

Own Shuttering Plates

By using our own shuttering plates, we prevent premature removal of shuttering (before 21 days), a common practice by competitors to reduce rental costs. This ensures better curing and structural integrity.

Proper Curing
11

On-Site Labor Accommodation

We provide accommodation near the construction site for laborers to minimize delays caused by absenteeism and ensure timely work starts every day. Consistent progress, every single day.

Zero Delays

We clean every area of the house thoroughly before handing it over to the client, ensuring a spotless finish. Move-in ready perfection.

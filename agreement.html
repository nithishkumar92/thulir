Terms & Conditions
  1. Commencement: Construction commences within 15 working days of Agreement signing payment (10%) and handing over of possession of the site.
  2. Timeline: The estimated duration is indicative. Delays caused by monsoon, client-directed changes, late payment, or government approvals are not counted toward the contractual timeline.
  3. Design Changes: Any changes to the approved drawings after work has begun will be assessed for additional cost and time. No changes are executed without a signed Change Order.
  4. Client Responsibilities: The client shall ensure: (a) clear site access at all times; (b) government plan approvals before commencement; (c) timely payments per the payment schedule; (d) an authorised person for on-site decisions.
  5. Material Procurement: Thulir Construction procures all agreed materials. The client may inspect material delivery. No third-party materials are to be introduced without mutual written consent.
  6. Work Supervision: A qualified site engineer appointed by Thulir Construction supervises all work. The client is entitled to visit the site at any time with prior notice. Daily/weekly progress updates will be shared via WhatsApp/ERP.
  7. Dispute Resolution: Any disputes shall first be resolved by mutual discussion within 30 days. If unresolved, disputes shall be referred to arbitration under the Arbitration & Conciliation Act, 1996. Jurisdiction: Sankari / Salem.
  8. Termination by Client: The client may terminate this agreement by giving 30 days' written notice. The client shall pay for all work completed, materials procured, and a cancellation fee of 5% of the remaining contract value.
  9. Termination by Contractor: Thulir Construction may suspend or terminate work if payment is overdue by more than 15 days beyond the due date, after issuing a written notice.
  10. Force Majeure: Neither party is liable for delays caused by acts of God, government orders, pandemic, or circumstances beyond reasonable control.
  11. Intellectual Property: Architectural drawings and structural designs remain the intellectual property of Thulir Construction unless otherwise agreed in writing.
  12. Safety: Thulir Construction ensures safe construction practices on site. The client and visitors must follow all site safety instructions when on site.

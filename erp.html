The Process

How Your Project Runs — Stage by Stage

1
Agreement & Project Setup
Once the agreement is signed, your project is created in our management system. Scope of work, material specifications, payment milestones, and the construction schedule are all entered and shared with you digitally.
Signed AgreementProject ScheduleMaterial Plan
2
Site Commencement & Foundation Work
Work begins with site clearing and foundation. You receive the first set of site photos. The engineer logs the foundation measurements, soil report notes, and materials used. Any Vastu recommendations are noted at this stage.
Site PhotosFoundation LogVastu Check
3
Structural Work — Ongoing Updates
As each floor is built, photo updates are sent at every key step — column casting, beam work, slab pour. Steel quantity used, cement bags consumed, and any design changes are documented in real time.
Slab Pour PhotosSteel LogChange Orders
4
Milestone Completion & Payment Trigger
When a milestone is reached (e.g. Ground Floor Slab complete), you receive a milestone report with photos and a payment invoice. You review, approve, and release the next stage payment. No surprise demands — everything tied to verified work.
Milestone ReportPhoto EvidenceInvoice
5
Finishing, Quality Check & Snag List
After plastering, tiling, painting, electrical, and plumbing are complete, a formal quality inspection is done. Any issues found are recorded in a snag list and resolved before handover. You are invited to participate in the final walkthrough.
Quality InspectionSnag ListClient Walkthrough
6
Handover & Documentation Package
At handover, you receive a complete documentation package — construction photos from every stage, material purchase records, warranty cards for fixtures, electrical schematic, plumbing layout, and warranty certificates. Your home story, documented.
Stage PhotosWarranty CardsElectrical & Plumbing LayoutMaterial Records
What You Get

Every Client Benefit, Explained

Site Photo Updates
Weekly construction photos shared via WhatsApp. Critical pours (foundations, slabs) get same-day photos. You watch your house rise even when you're 500 km away.
Live Milestone Tracker
Every stage of construction is a milestone — Foundation, G/F Slab, Brickwork, Roof, Finishing, Handover. You always know exactly which stage is active and what's next.
Material Purchase Log
Every purchase is recorded — steel (by kg), cement (by bag), bricks (by count), tiles (by box). No mystery about what went into your home. Bills are available on request.
Milestone-Tied Billing
You never pay for work that hasn't been done. Each invoice is linked to a completed, photographed milestone — exactly as agreed in the payment schedule.
Issue & Change Tracking
Any design change, site issue, or unexpected condition is logged immediately with cause and resolution. Nothing is hidden. You are notified and every change order is in writing.
Schedule & Delay Alerts
If monsoon, material shortage, or any factor causes a delay, you receive a revised schedule immediately — not after you discover it yourself during a site visit.
Dedicated Site Engineer
A qualified civil engineer is on-site daily. Their daily log, RCC checking notes, and quality observations are part of your project record.
Quality Checkpoints
12 formal quality checkpoints across the construction cycle — from soil inspection before foundation to electrical load testing before handover. Each is signed off and documented.
Permanent Project Archive
Your complete project file — drawings, photos, purchase records, change orders, inspection notes — is archived and available to you permanently, even years after handover.

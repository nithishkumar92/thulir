Our Facility

Anti-Termite Treatment

We carry out anti-termite treatment after soil filling up to the basement level to ensure long-term protection against termites.

Detailed Agreement Copy

We provide a comprehensive agreement copy to clients before starting work, including all specifications, details of work, and the payment schedule. This avoids unwanted confusion and ensures transparency.

Structural Analysis and Elevation

We deliver a detailed structural analysis and share the exterior elevation photo as discussed with the client before completing the basement work.

Water Leakage Test

We conduct a thorough water leakage test in sunken areas and RCC water tanks on raw concrete surfaces before any plastering or water treatment. After identifying leakage spots, we perform 'pressure PU grout' to arrest the leakage, followed by regular plastering work on the surface.

Mini Mixer Machine for Plaster Mortar

We use a mini mixer machine to prepare plaster mortar, ensuring proper and consistent cement mixing for superior quality.

Use of Vibrators

We ensure the use of vibrators for all major concrete work to avoid honeycombs and achieve uniform settlement of concrete in the formwork.

Mini Power Trowel Machine

We use a mini power trowel machine across plastered surfaces to ensure uniform finishes and an aesthetically pleasing look.

Putty Spraying Machine

We utilize a putty spraying machine with a 5-foot-long trowel to level surfaces, eliminating unwanted undulations on walls and achieving a smooth and level finish.

Crane for Material Shifting

Our crane, operable up to 5 floors, eliminates labor wastage and saves time in shifting materials to higher floors.

Own Shuttering Plates

By using our own shuttering plates, we prevent premature removal of shuttering (before 21 days), a common practice by competitors to reduce rental costs. This ensures better curing and structural integrity.

On-Site Labor Accommodation

We provide accommodation near the construction site for laborers to minimize delays caused by absenteeism and ensure timely work starts every day.

Clean Handovers

We clean every area of the house thoroughly before handing it over to the client, ensuring a spotless finish.
